# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to help management monitor performance across stores and product categories.

## Business Objective
- Track overall sales and profitability  
- Identify top and underperforming stores  
- Understand category contribution and sales trends  

## Data
- **Sales transactions:** revenue, cost, quantity  
- **Store master data:** region, store name  
- **Product master data:** category  
- **Calendar table:** for time intelligence  

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales  
- Gross Profit & Margin %  
- MTD & YTD Sales  
- YoY Growth  

## Key Insights
- Electronics drives the highest revenue but has lower margins than accessories.  
- A small number of stores contribute a significant share of total sales.  
- Sales growth is positive YoY, but margin pressure exists during high-volume months.  

## Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategy for low-margin categories.  
- Review store-level assortment for underperforming locations.  
- Align inventory planning with seasonal sales trends.
